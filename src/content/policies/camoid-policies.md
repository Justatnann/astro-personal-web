### 1. Information Collection and Use

Camoid is designed with privacy as a core principle. To provide the core functionality of transforming your mobile device into a studio camera, the Application requires specific device permissions.

The video feed captured by your camera is processed entirely on your device and transmitted directly to your paired personal computer via your local network (Wi-Fi). **We do not record, store, or transmit your video feed to any external cloud servers or third parties.**
